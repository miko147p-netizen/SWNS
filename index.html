Our Story

The bakery, in our own words

At Sweet and Savoury Bakery we take pride in offering a diverse range of baked goods, including delicate pastries, delightful macarons, and irresistible Baklawa. Our biscuit shop boasts an array of cookies and dry cakes, perfect for every craving. If you're in the mood for something savory, try our delicious veg puffs or savor the softness of our naan khatai. Sweet and Savoury Bakery is managed by former 5 Star Hotel chefs and it offers mouth watering sweet and savoury delicacies.
